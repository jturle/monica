Connect an agent

monica exposes a Chrome DevTools Protocol endpoint at http://127.0.0.1:9222 . Pages your agent opens become panes here — watch them live and take over anytime. Use the Local / LAN toggle (top-right) to reach it from another machine.

Recommended — agent-browser-mcp

The jturle fork of agent-browser-mcp drives monica over CDP and tags each agent session, so every session gets its own pane(s) and closing a session clears them. Add it to your MCP client (e.g. Claude Code's .mcp.json):

.mcp.json — agent-browser-mcp (jturle fork)

            

            
AGENT_BROWSER_CDP follows the Local / LAN toggle above — copy it as-is.
